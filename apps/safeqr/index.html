    SafeQR — Secure QR Code Scanner

    Privacy & Cybersecurity

    SafeQR is a privacy-first QR code scanner that analyzes URLs in real time before you open them. Whether you’re scanning a restaurant menu, product code, or random flyer, SafeQR acts as your personal bodyguard—keeping you safe from malicious redirects, phishing links, and data-harvesting pages.

    Key Features

    • Sandboxed Scanning: Each scan runs inside a secure environment to isolate untrusted links.
    • Real-Time Threat Checks: URLs are analyzed using trusted APIs like VirusTotal, URLScan, and OpenPhish.
    • Instant Safety Ratings: Clear visual indicators show whether a link is safe, suspicious, or blocked.
    • Offline Mode: Basic scanning works even without an internet connection.
    • Privacy-Preserving: No user tracking, analytics, or data sharing—your scans stay yours.

    Base Package (Free)

    • Unlimited QR scans with secure sandbox isolation.
    • Basic threat detection via SafeQR’s local rule set.
    • Offline QR decoding and preview display.
    • Access to community-reported suspicious link data.
    • Dark mode and privacy-only UI—no ads, no telemetry.

    Perfect for casual users who want safe, clean QR scanning without sacrificing privacy.

    Premium Package (Pro)

    • Advanced cloud-based scanning with VirusTotal, URLScan.io, and OpenPhish integrations.
    • SmartLink AI that explains why a link may be unsafe.
    • Device-level protection—intercepts unsafe URLs before opening in Safari/Chrome.
    • History analytics: view trends in your scan data.
    • Priority support and early access to new modules (e.g., document scan validation, NFC link checks).

    Ideal for professionals, cybersecurity enthusiasts, and power users who demand total trust in their tools.

    Upcoming Features

    • Browser extension for instant link vetting.
    • Enterprise dashboard for team QR safety metrics.
    • Integration with Apple Shortcuts & Android Intents.

    SafeQR is designed to make digital interactions safer for everyone—whether you’re scanning a code on a café table or a business presentation. Download soon on iOS.
